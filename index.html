Real-Time Voice-Based Speaker Recognition

Deep learning meets voice. Identify who's speaking in just 15 seconds.

About the System

This web application leverages the power of deep learning to identify individuals by their unique voice signatures. At its core lies the ECAPA-TDNN model from SpeechBrain, a state-of-the-art neural network architecture for speaker recognition.

The system captures a short audio sample from the speaker, generates an embedding (a compact representation of voice features), and compares it with pre-enrolled samples to determine the most similar speaker in real time.
